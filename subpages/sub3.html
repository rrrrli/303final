Validation over time for boy and girls






This subdomain of friendship quality examines how often friends can provide emotional support, understanding, and care. Having close friends who are good at providing validation can make adolescents more resilient in face of adversity. Little is known about how validation differs in boys’ and girls’ friendships. However, some research has shown that adolescent girls tend to show more helping behaviors in general peer relationships than boys.
